01 / ABOUT
Bhumika Raj
WSP · NYC
I get regional rail projects built — data, stakeholders, funding, all of it. Lately that's pulled me into a second lane: helping public agencies make sense of AI before they're asked to regulate it.
Embedded at MTA's Office of Statewide Planning — MARC Commuter Rail, TIP/STIP programming, wayfinding across Maryland. Before that: Amtrak's Gateway Program and Mumbai Metro Line 3. Civil engineering (CEPT), city & regional planning (Rutgers), now working through a data analytics degree on the side. Also WSP's go-to on AI — piloting an automation tool with FEMA and writing the firm's adoption playbook.
